\opening{Dear Editor,}

I am pleased to submit our manuscript entitled ``Temporal Attention-Enhanced Lightweight CNN for Student Engagement Detection on Edge Devices'' for consideration for publication in \textit{Electronics}.

This paper proposes a novel framework combining:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item A multi-scale Channel-Spatial Attention Module (CSAM) with parallel 3×3, 5×5, 7×7 convolutions and adaptive fusion.
    \item A BiLSTM-based temporal aggregation module for video-level engagement prediction.
    \item Deployment-oriented evaluation on edge hardware.
\end{enumerate}

Key results:
\begin{itemize}
    \item FER2013: 70.5\% accuracy (5-fold CV)
    \item CK+ cross-dataset: 71.9\% accuracy
    \item DAiSEE: 94.8\% video-level accuracy (vs 61.2\% frame-level)
\end{itemize}

The temporal aggregation module dramatically improves video-level performance, demonstrating that temporal modeling is essential for engagement detection. The complete pipeline runs at 53 fps on GPU, suitable for real-time classroom deployment.

Both authors have approved the manuscript and declare no conflicts of interest. I look forward to your decision.

\closing{Sincerely,}
