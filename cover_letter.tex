\begin{letter}{Editor-in-Chief \\ Applied Sciences \\ MDPI}

\opening{Dear Editor,}

I am pleased to submit our manuscript entitled ``Deep Learning for Student Engagement Detection: A Lightweight CNN Framework with Channel-Spatial Attention for Educational Environments'' for consideration for publication in \textit{Applied Sciences}.

This paper proposes a lightweight deep learning framework integrating MobileNetV3 with a novel Channel-Spatial Attention Module (CSAM) for real-time student engagement recognition from facial expressions. The main contributions are:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item A lightweight CSAM module designed for mobile architectures, achieving 1.3--5.0\% accuracy improvement with only 0.01M additional parameters.
    \item Comprehensive evaluation across three datasets: FER2013 (70.5\% accuracy), CK+ cross-dataset (71.9\%), and DAiSEE (61.2\% binary).
    \item Deployment-oriented evaluation including parameter count, FLOPs, inference latency, and energy consumption.
\end{enumerate}

The MobileNetV3+CSAM model achieves competitive accuracy with only 1.26M parameters and 53 fps, making it suitable for edge deployment. Both authors have approved the manuscript and declare no conflicts of interest.

I look forward to your decision.

\closing{Sincerely,}
